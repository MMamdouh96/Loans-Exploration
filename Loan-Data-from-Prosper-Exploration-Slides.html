Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation i want to look at the relationship between loans amount, IncomeRange, IncomeVerifiable.

Dataset Overview¶

The data contains data about loans it's 113937 rows and 81 columns. we have many important columns (LoanStatus, BorrowerRate, ListingCategory (numeric), IncomeRange, IncomeVerifiable, StatedMonthlyIncome)

Distribution of Stated Monthly Income¶

For the Distribution of Stated Monthly Income, we can see that we have found an outlier in the distribution with Stated Monthly Income 1750000 that is really big number comparing to the all data so we have filtered it out, to see the real distribution more clear. Form the distribution of Stated Monthly Income we can notice that the average of monthly income is around 5000 and that which means the people who get paid around 5000 has higher percentage to take loans.
